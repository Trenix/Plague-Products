Code.Net

Enter a world in 2090 where all systems have been digitalized. The government is training their workforce to combat hackers through a social deduction game. Connect together with friends and family on the application and score as many points as possible using abilities and deception.

Mobile Application
Features
  • No cheating or any requirement to close your eyes.
  • No moving pieces that can be damaged or lost.
  • Full automation so you can enjoy more games and prevent human error.
Point System
Features
  • Every action you make matters and follows you to the next match.
  • Punishes only the player that plays incorrectly.
  • Side objectives exist to score more points and keep each match from being redundant.
Probability
Features
  • Players are provided logs which may or may not be accurate to retain uncertainty throughout the game.
  • There is no player elimination which means no one has to sit out of the game until the next match.
  • Hackers are not so easily uncovered and can hide behind probability to appear credible.
Actions
Features
  • Many unique actions that would be impossible to preform if using physical cards.
  • More actions can always be added and previous ones can be properly balanced overtime.
  • Preform actions from the comfort of your own phone with no intrusion or sharing of your device.
